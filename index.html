About Us

At Leyan Pharmaceuticals, we are a trusted partner for wholesalers across Ethiopia, providing a seamless and reliable distribution network for high-quality pharmaceutical products. Our services are designed to meet the unique needs of wholesalers, ensuring they have access to the essential medications and medical supplies required to serve their markets effectively.

At Leyan Pharmaceuticals, we believe in integrity, transparency, and a customer-centric approach. Our team is dedicated to delivering exceptional service, fostering trust with our clients, and contributing to the advancement of healthcare in Ethiopia. Our Products

Amoxicillin

Amoxicillin is an antibiotic used to treat a variety of bacterial infections.

Azithromycin

Azithromycin is an antibiotic that helps treat infections caused by bacteria, including respiratory and skin infections.

Ceftriaxone

Ceftriaxone is a broad-spectrum antibiotic used for treating various infections, including pneumonia, meningitis, and urinary tract infections.

Insulin

Insulin is a hormone used to manage blood sugar levels in individuals with diabetes.

Metronidazole

Metronidazole is an antibiotic and antiprotozoal medication used to treat infections such as bacterial vaginosis, gastrointestinal infections, and more.

Paracetamol

Paracetamol (also known as acetaminophen) is a pain reliever and fever reducer commonly used to treat mild to moderate pain.
